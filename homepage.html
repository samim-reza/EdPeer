Why Choose EdPeer?

Our platform offers unique features designed to enhance your learning experience.

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Connect with peers who have expertise in the topics you're struggling with, and share your knowledge in return.

Real-Time Communication

Chat, use interactive whiteboards, and collaborate on code in real-time for an effective learning experience.

Optional Anonymity

Tutors can choose to remain anonymous, removing barriers and focusing purely on knowledge sharing.

Credit-Based System

Earn credits by teaching others and use them to get help when you need it, creating a balanced ecosystem.

Code Editor

Collaborate on code with syntax highlighting and real-time editing, perfect for programming help.

Interactive Whiteboard

Explain complex concepts visually using our interactive whiteboard with drawing and text tools.

How EdPeer Works

Getting started with EdPeer is simple and straightforward.

1

Create an Account

Sign up and create your profile with your areas of expertise and topics you need help with.

2

Request Help

Post a specific topic you need help with and our system will match you with suitable peers.

3

Connect & Learn

Join a real-time session with your matched peer using our interactive tools.

4

Pay It Forward

Share your knowledge in your areas of expertise to earn credits for future sessions.
